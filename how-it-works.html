How It Works

Support, Guidance, & Services

Step 1


Complete the form

Step 2

An email response will be sent within 48 business hours.
A Win2ition™ representative will schedule a live conversation.
If accepted, the process will continue.

Step 3

An advocate is assigned. The top 3 areas of stress; personally or professionally will be evaluated, agreed and focused on.
A meeting is then scheduled to determine needs & plan development.

Step 4

A Win2ition™ team is assigned to provide support in accomplishing the plan.
(A Win2ition™ team consists of advisers, partners, volunteers, sponsors, & donors)
